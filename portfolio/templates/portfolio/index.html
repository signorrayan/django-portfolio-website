ABOUT ME

Hi there!


I'm a full-stack web developer based in New York City with experience building apps in React / Redux and Ruby on Rails.


My favorite part of programming is the never-ending sense that there's always something new to master --- a secret language feature, an established design pattern, an emerging library or -- most importantly -- a different way of looking at a problem.
